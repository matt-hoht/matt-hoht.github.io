About Me.

Hi! I am Matt. I am a UX Designer and Front-End Developer currently living in San Diego. I spend my free time playing volleyball and video games. Persona 5 is my favorite game when it comes to UX and UI design. The designs for each menu screen is designed differently with each shop keeper's characteristics in mind making the mundane tasks of shopping for weapons and recovery items in this game feel fun and stylish different than other traditional JRPG. I am passionate about helping people to solve a problem through Human-Centered Design using UX research and strategies.
