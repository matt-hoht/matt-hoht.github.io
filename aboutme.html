About Me.


Hello I am Matt. I am a solution focused, detail-oriented entry level UX Designer graduated from University of California, San Diego. I was previously an event coordinator and I found User Experience Design share many aspects of event coordinating such as, performing market research, human-centered design and improving based on feedback. I decided to pursue UX Design because UX Design provides me a channel to express my creativity and be able to connect with people around me. I want to make changes to the world by solving problem through Human-Centered Design principles and continue to challenge myself to become a better UX Designer.
