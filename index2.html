I am Matt Ho.

My first time realizing how UX/UI design can really shape a user's experience was my first playthrough of Persona 5. Seeing how the developers can turn a usually mundane video game menu into something so stylish and ties back to the theme of the game sparked my interest in UX/UI design.

I have graduated from University of California, San Diego with a degree in Cognitive Science specializing in Human Computer Interaction. Currently I am looking for a position where I can continue to learn more about UX and UI design from other designer as well as honing my skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript.
